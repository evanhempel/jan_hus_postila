This is a summary of Jan Hus's Postila, a collection of sermons organized by the liturgical calendar. The original Czech text contains 60 sermons covering Advent through Trinity Sunday and special occasions like church consecrations.

- **Structure**: 
  - 57 Sunday sermons following the liturgical year
  - 2 sermons for church consecration
  - 1 additional sermon ("Dodavek")
- **Liturgical Coverage**:
  - Advent to Trinity season (weeks 1-24 after Trinity)
  - Major feasts: Christmas, Easter, Pentecost
  - Special occasions like Shrovetide and church dedication
- **Additional Materials**:
  - Preface and introduction
  - Editorial notes by J.B. Jeschke
