This summary provides a concise overview of Jan Hus' sermon on John 8:46-59, preached during the Fifth Sunday of Lent. It focuses on Jesus' confrontation with the Jewish leaders and the profound truths He reveals.

### Key Content:
- **Jesus' Challenge to His Accusers**: Jesus questions who among them can convict Him of sin, asserting His purity and divine origin.
- **Jewish Leaders' Accusations**: The Jews accuse Jesus of being a Samaritan and having a demon, rejecting His teachings.
- **Jesus' Response**: Jesus denies having a demon and emphasizes His reverence for God the Father, contrasting His humility with their pride.
- **Eternal Life Through His Word**: Jesus declares that those who keep His word will never see eternal death, a statement the Jews misunderstand.
- **Jesus' Eternal Nature**: He affirms His pre-existence before Abraham, claiming divinity and eternal existence.
- **Jesus' Humility**: Despite their hostility, Jesus avoids confrontation, exemplifying humility and restraint.

### Key Quote:
> "Truly, truly, I say to you, if anyone keeps my word, he will never see death."  
*(John 8:51)*
