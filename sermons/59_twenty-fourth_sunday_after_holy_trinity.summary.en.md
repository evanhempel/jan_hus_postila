This is a summary of Jan Hus’s sermon on the Twenty-Fourth Sunday After Holy Trinity, based on Matthew chapter 9, verses 18-22. Jan Hus reflects on Jesus healing a woman with an issue of blood and raising a ruler’s daughter, emphasizing themes of faith, humility, and Christ’s divine power.

### Key Content:
- **Jesus’ Healing Power**: Jan Hus recounts how Jesus healed a woman who had suffered from a hemorrhage for twelve years and raised the daughter of a Jewish ruler from the dead.
  - The woman’s faith led her to touch Jesus’ garment, believing she would be healed.
  - The ruler, Jairus, approached Jesus with humility, asking Him to heal his daughter.
- **Faith and Healing**: Jan Hus highlights the importance of faith, noting that the woman’s trust in Christ resulted in her healing.
  - Jesus affirms her faith, saying, “Your faith has made you well.”
- **Christ’s Humility**: Jan Hus contrasts Jesus’ humility with the pride of religious leaders, noting how Jesus willingly went to heal the sick despite His divine status.
- **Spiritual Lessons**:
  - Faith in Christ is essential for spiritual and physical healing.
  - Humility and trust in God’s power are virtues that believers should emulate.

### Key Quote:
> “Doufaj, dcero! viera tvá tě zdrávu učinila.”  
> *Translation*: “Take heart, daughter! Your faith has made you well.”  
> *(Matthew 9:22)*
