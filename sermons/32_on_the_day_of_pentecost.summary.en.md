This sermon by Jan Hus reflects on the significance of the Holy Spirit, drawing from John chapter 14, verses 23-31. Hus emphasizes the importance of loving Christ and keeping His commandments as the true evidence of faith. The sermon also highlights the role of the Holy Spirit in guiding believers and the promise of eternal communion with God.

## Key Points:
1. **Loving Christ**: True love for Christ is demonstrated by keeping His commandments.
2. **Role of the Holy Spirit**:
   - The Holy Spirit teaches believers and reminds them of Christ's words.
   - It brings comfort and peace to those who follow Christ.
3. **Eternal Communion**: Those who love Christ and keep His word will be loved by the Father, and God will dwell with them eternally.
4. **Christ’s Mission**: Christ’s obedience to the Father’s will is a model for believers, emphasizing the importance of fulfilling God’s commandments.
5. **Peace from Christ**: The peace Christ offers is not like worldly peace but is a deeper spiritual peace that transcends earthly struggles.

## Key Quote:
> "Ač kto miluje mě, řeč mú zachová"  
> ("If anyone loves me, he will keep my word.")  
> — John 14:23
