This is a summary of Jan Hus's sermon based on Luke chapter 24, verses 36-47, focusing on Jesus' appearance to His disciples after His resurrection. Jan Hus emphasizes the importance of faith, repentance, and the fulfillment of Scripture through Christ's suffering and resurrection.

### Key Points:
- **Jesus Appears to His Disciples**: Jesus appears to His disciples after His resurrection, bringing them peace and reassuring them not to fear.
- **Proof of Resurrection**: Jesus shows His hands and feet, proving He is not a spirit but has a physical body.
- **Fulfillment of Scripture**: Jesus explains that His suffering, death, and resurrection fulfilled the prophecies in the Law of Moses, the Prophets, and the Psalms.
- **Repentance and Forgiveness**: Jesus commands His disciples to preach repentance and forgiveness of sins in His name to all nations.

### Key Quote:
> "Tato jsú slova, kteráž sem mluvil vám, když sem ještě byl s vámi, že musějí naplněny býti všecky věci, kteráž psány jsú v zákoně Mojžiešově a v prorociech a v psalmiech o mně."  
> ("These are the words I spoke to you while I was still with you, that everything written about me in the Law of Moses, the Prophets, and the Psalms must be fulfilled.")
