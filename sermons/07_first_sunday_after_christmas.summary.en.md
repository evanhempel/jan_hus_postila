This summary highlights key points from Jan Hus's sermon on the First Sunday After Christmas, based on Luke chapter 2, verses 33-40. It focuses on the events surrounding Jesus's early life and the reactions of those who encountered Him.

### Key Points:
1. **Joseph and Mary marveled** at the prophecies and events surrounding Jesus's birth.
2. **Simeon prophesied** that Jesus would cause both the rise and fall of many in Israel and that a sword would pierce Mary's soul.
3. **Anna the Prophetess** served God devoutly in the temple and testified about Jesus.
4. **Jesus grew in wisdom and grace**, fulfilling the prophecies and demonstrating His divine nature.

### Key Quote:
> "He has been destined for the falling and the rising of many in Israel, and to be a sign that will be opposed."
